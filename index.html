About RADE Analytics

RADE Analytics is a state-of-the-art cannabis testing laboratory committed to providing accurate, reliable, and comprehensive analytical services to the cannabis industry.

Our ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited facility utilizes advanced instrumentation and rigorous quality control procedures to ensure the highest standards of testing accuracy and reliability.

We specialize in complete safety and potency testing, helping cannabis businesses meet regulatory requirements and ensure product quality for consumers.

Cannabinoid Analysis

Comprehensive cannabinoid profiling using HPLC technology to determine THC, CBD, CBG, and other cannabinoids with precision.

Safety Testing

Complete safety panel including pesticides, heavy metals, microbials, mycotoxins, and residual solvents testing.

Moisture & Terpenes

Moisture content analysis and comprehensive terpene profiling to ensure product quality and compliance.
